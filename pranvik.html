The Problem & Solution

Why Pranvik exists

Millions of small dairy farmers earn less than fair value while consumers pay more for milk. Middlemen take up to 40% of the price. We fix that.

❌

The Problem

Farmers sell at low rates to agents. Consumers pay high prices. No transparency. No trust. Both sides lose.

✅

Our Solution

Pranvik is a digital platform that connects farmers directly to nearby consumers — fair prices, daily fresh milk, easy payments via UPI.
